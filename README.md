# 📊 Power BI SuperStore Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Power BI SuperStore Sales Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence solution designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, shipping modes, payment methods, and regional sales trends. The dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions by providing key insights into retail business operations.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 📈 Sales Performance Analysis

* Total Sales Overview
* Sales Trend Analysis (Monthly & Year-over-Year)
* Regional Sales Distribution
* State-wise Sales and Profit Analysis

### 💰 Profit Analysis

* Total Profit Tracking
* Monthly Profit Comparison
* Profit Distribution Across Categories

### 🛍️ Product Insights

* Sales by Category
* Sales by Sub-Category
* Top Performing Products

### 👥 Customer Insights

* Sales by Customer Segment

  * Consumer
  * Corporate
  * Home Office

### 🚚 Shipping Analysis

* Sales by Ship Mode

  * Standard Class
  * Second Class
  * First Class
  * Same Day

### 💳 Payment Mode Analysis

* Cash on Delivery (COD)
* Online Payments
* Card Payments

### 🌎 Geographic Analysis

* Interactive Map Visualization
* State-wise Sales & Profit Distribution

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Monitor overall sales performance.
* Identify profitable product categories.
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Track shipping and payment preferences.
* Compare Year-over-Year sales and profit trends.
* Support strategic business decision-making.

---

## 📈 Insights Generated

* Consumer segment contributes the highest share of sales.
* Standard Class is the most preferred shipping mode.
* Office Supplies category generates the highest sales.
* Phones, Chairs, and Binders are top-selling sub-categories.
* Significant sales growth observed during the last quarter of the year.
